About the Institute of Digital Risk

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute preparing specialists for digital, cyber and technology risk roles in regulated, high-consequence environments. We focus on the advanced skills, governance frameworks and compliance capabilities required by critical infrastructure, finance and regulated technology sectors.

Through our UK university partnership, IDR combines academic insight with real-world projects so learners graduate with practical experience in risk assessment, security architecture and incident governance. We embed applied collaboration with industry partners to ensure participants deliver impact from day one.

Our Model

IDR operates through a unique model combining education, innovation and advisory services to develop talent and deploy solutions for modern digital risk challenges.

Academy

Training programs and professional bootcamps that prepare students and professionals to work in cybersecurity, AI governance and digital risk management.

Innovation

Research initiatives focused on emerging risk models, AI safety frameworks and governance tools for complex technology ecosystems.

Advisory

Helping organisations implement frameworks like NIST, ISO 27001, and NIS2, as well as security standards and digital governance models for regulated sectors.
